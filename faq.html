Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about how Assistio works, what you get for $150/month, and what to expect when you start.

1. What is Assistio?

Assistio is an AI-powered customer service and sales assistant. It handles customer questions, qualifies leads, books appointments, and routes complex issues to your team across channels like website chat, WhatsApp, email, Instagram, and Facebook.

2. What do I get for $150 per month?

For $150/month you get:

  • One AI assistant connected to your website chat widget.
  • Integration with WhatsApp, email, Instagram, and Facebook messaging.
  • AI answers to your most common questions, based on your own content and FAQs.
  • Automated flows to capture leads and book appointments.
  • Automatic escalation to a human agent when the AI is not confident.
  • Basic conversation history and reporting.

There are no hidden per-user fees at this stage.

3. Do you charge a setup fee?

Yes. We charge a one-time setup fee of $150 per business.

During setup, we:

  • Connect your channels (chat, WhatsApp, email, Instagram, Facebook).
  • Import or create your main FAQs and answers.
  • Configure your flows (lead capture, handoff rules, appointment booking).
  • Test everything with you before going live.

4. Who is Assistio for?

Assistio is built for:

  • Service businesses (clinics, agencies, real estate, restaurants, etc.).
  • Online businesses that receive many repetitive questions.
  • Teams that want faster responses without building a big support team.

If you want one system to handle first-line support and lead qualification across multiple channels, Assistio is designed for you.

5. Which channels do you support?

At this stage we focus on:

  • Website chat widget.
  • WhatsApp.
  • Email.
  • Instagram and Facebook messaging.

You can start with one channel and add others when you are ready.

6. How long does it take to go live?

Most clients can go live in a few days, depending on how fast we receive your content.

A typical setup process looks like this:

  1. We have a short call to understand your business and main use cases.
  2. You share your existing FAQs, documentation, and links.
  3. We connect your channels and configure your flows.
  4. You test the assistant in a safe environment.
  5. When you are happy, we turn it on for real customers.

7. Do I need to write code or manage the AI myself?

No. We handle the technical integrations and AI configuration for you. You only need to:

  • Approve the answers and flows.
  • Tell us what to prioritize (sales, support, booking, etc.).
  • Update your FAQs and content when your business changes.

8. What happens if the AI doesn’t know the answer?

When the AI is not confident, or when a customer asks to speak to a human, Assistio:

  • Stops trying to guess the answer.
  • Escalates the conversation to your chosen channel (email, WhatsApp, etc.).
  • Sends the full conversation history so your team has context.

Your human team can then reply directly, and the user stays in the same channel.

9. Can I see what the AI is replying?

Yes. You can review:

  • Conversation history by channel (chat, WhatsApp, email, Instagram, Facebook).
  • Messages where the AI handled the case.
  • Messages where a human stepped in.

This helps you improve your FAQs and rules over time.

10. Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. For monthly billing, you can cancel before the next billing date and you will not be charged again. There are no long-term contracts at this stage unless we sign a custom agreement with you.

11. How does billing work?

Our current structure is:

  • Subscription: $150 per month per business.
  • Setup: $150 one-time per business.

Invoices are issued monthly. If you are an agency and want to onboard multiple clients, we can discuss custom terms.

12. How do you handle data and privacy?

We take data protection seriously:

  • Conversations are encrypted in transit.
  • We do not sell your data or your customers’ data.
  • We only use your data to power your assistant and improve its performance for your business.
  • You can request data deletion for your account.

If you need more details, we can provide a summary of our data handling and security practices.

13. How do I get started?

You can book a call using the button on this website or send us a message through the Contact page. We will walk you through your use case, show you what Assistio can do, and, if it is a good fit, start your setup and trial.
