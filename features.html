The Assistio Platform

Tools for the complete customer conversation lifecycle – from the first message to the booked appointment and follow-up.

Engage. Automate. Manage.

Assistio combines an AI agent, multi-channel messaging, and automation behind the scenes, so your team can focus on the conversations that really need a human.

Engage

AI that meets your customers on your website, WhatsApp, email, Instagram, and Facebook – with one consistent brain behind every response.

Automate

No-code flows that turn conversations into leads, bookings, and internal actions without extra manual work.

Manage

Dashboards, history, and multi-client workspaces that keep you in control of every interaction.

Engage – One assistant across all your channels

Customers don’t care which channel they use. Assistio keeps answers fast and consistent everywhere.

Website AI chat widget

Add a modern, branded widget to your site with a single snippet and turn visitors into conversations.

  • Floating chat widget that works on desktop and mobile.
  • Custom colors, branding, and welcome messages per business.
  • Optional pre-chat form to capture name, email, or phone.
  • AI answers questions using your own FAQs, policies, and content.

WhatsApp, Instagram & Facebook messaging

Connect your messaging channels so your assistant can reply to DMs and chats just like on your website.

  • Support for WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook messaging.
  • Same AI model and rules across all channels – no duplicate setup.
  • Channel-specific routing and escalation rules per client.
  • All customer messages are stored in a single conversation history.

Smart email replies

Let Assistio handle repetitive email requests and free your team to focus on complex cases.

  • Connect a support email address per business.
  • AI drafts and sends replies for common questions using your FAQs.
  • Complex or risky emails are flagged and forwarded to a human with a suggested draft.
  • Full email history is stored alongside chat and messaging conversations.

Human handoff when needed

When AI shouldn’t answer, Assistio knows when to step aside and bring a human in.

  • Escalate based on low confidence, specific keywords, or lead criteria.
  • Send context-rich notifications to your team (email, WhatsApp, or other channels you choose).
  • Your staff reply from their usual tools; Assistio relays the message back to the customer.
  • Customer stays in the same channel; no forced channel switch.

Automate – Flows behind every conversation

Conversations don’t end at an answer. Use Assistio to route leads, book calls, and trigger internal workflows.

Automated appointment booking

Let AI book calls, demos, or visits for you – without juggling calendars over chat.

  • Connect to your preferred booking tool (e.g. Calendly or similar).
  • AI only offers times when you are actually available.
  • Automatic confirmation messages and reminders to reduce no-shows.
  • Bookings are attached to the customer record for future follow-up.

Lead capture & CRM-friendly data

Make sure every serious inquiry becomes a qualified lead with structured data.

  • Capture name, contact details, and key qualification questions from chat or messaging.
  • Standardize lead fields so they are easy to push into your CRM or pipelines.
  • Route high-intent leads to your sales team instantly.
  • Use tags and flows to differentiate between support and sales conversations.

No-code flows powered by your automation backbone

Under the hood, Assistio uses no-code flows so you can adjust logic without rebuilding everything.

  • Trigger flows on events like “new conversation”, “lead captured”, or “booking confirmed”.
  • Send notifications to internal tools (email, Slack/Teams, etc.).
  • Update external systems (CRMs, sheets, internal APIs) based on conversation events.
  • Different flows per client workspace so each business behaves differently.

Business rules & guardrails

Keep your assistant within your policies and tone, even when conversations get complex.

  • Define which topics AI is allowed to answer and which must always go to a human.
  • Configure tone of voice and canned responses for sensitive questions.
  • Set business hours and out-of-hours behavior per client.
  • Update rules without redeploying or changing code.

Manage – Visibility and control for every client

See what your assistant is doing, where humans stepped in, and how each client is performing.

Conversation dashboard

One place to review conversations across all channels for every business you run on Assistio.

  • Filter by channel, status (AI-handled vs escalated), and date range.
  • Open any conversation to see full history and customer details.
  • Spot recurring questions and gaps in your knowledge base.
  • Use insights to improve your FAQs and flows over time.

Basic analytics that actually matter

Focus on the numbers that show real impact, not vanity metrics.

  • Conversation volume and channel breakdown per client.
  • Share of conversations resolved by AI alone.
  • Escalation rate and response times for human handoffs.
  • Top topics and intents, so you know what to optimize next.

Multi-client workspaces for agencies

Manage multiple businesses from one place, with clean separation and centralized oversight.

  • Dedicated workspace per client with its own channels, FAQs, and rules.
  • Isolated data and configuration for each business.
  • Global view for your agency to track activity and performance across all clients.
  • Fast onboarding for new clients using the same underlying platform.

Per-client configuration

Make each assistant truly feel like part of your client’s brand.

  • Set business name, logo, brand color, and tone of voice.
  • Configure escalation paths and business hours per workspace.
  • Store channel credentials securely for each client.
  • Control which features are enabled on a client-by-client basis.
