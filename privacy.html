How we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Assistio.

This Privacy Policy explains how Assistio (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website [https://your-domain] and use our services.

  • Email: [privacy@yourcompany.com]

1. What information we collect

1.1 Information you provide to us directly

When you interact with our website or contact us, you may provide:

  • Contact details (e.g. name, email address, phone number).
  • Company details (e.g. company name, website, role).
  • Messages you send via contact forms or email (e.g. questions, feedback, project details).
  • Information submitted when booking a demo or requesting a trial.

If you become a customer, we may also collect:

  • Billing information (e.g. invoicing details, VAT ID).
  • Account details for your team members (e.g. names, work emails).

We do not ask for or intentionally collect sensitive personal information (such as national ID numbers, health data, or similar) through the website.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our website and services.
  • Respond to your inquiries, demo requests, and support questions.
  • Set up and manage your account if you become a customer.
  • Send you important notifications about our service (e.g. updates, security alerts, billing).
  • Analyze usage of our website to understand what is working and improve the experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Legal bases for processing (EU/EEA or UK)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, we process your personal data under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: when we need to provide services you requested or fulfill our agreement with you.
  • Legitimate interests: for example, to improve our services, prevent fraud, or secure our systems, where these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Consent: where required (e.g. certain marketing communications or optional cookies), we rely on your consent. You can withdraw it at any time.
  • Legal obligations: where we are required to keep certain records or share information with authorities.

4. Cookies and similar technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Ensure the website functions properly.
  • Remember your preferences.
  • Analyze how visitors use the site (e.g. via analytics tools).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some parts of the website may not function correctly if you disable cookies.

If we use third-party analytics or tracking tools, they may set their own cookies in accordance with their privacy policies.

5. How we share information

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: third parties that help us operate our website and services (e.g. hosting providers, analytics services, email providers, payment processors).
  • Professional advisors: such as lawyers, accountants, or auditors when necessary.
  • Authorities: when we are legally required to do so or to protect our legal rights, users, or the public.

When we work with service providers, we require them to only use your data to provide services to us and to protect your information.

If we ever sell, merge, or restructure our business, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will take steps to ensure your rights remain protected.

6. International transfers

Depending on where you are located and where our service providers are based, your information may be transferred and processed in countries outside of your own.

Where required by law, we will take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers (for example, using standard contractual clauses approved by regulators).

7. How we protect your information

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access.

However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted over the internet.

As part of our service, we may use reputable third-party infrastructure and AI providers. We choose providers that implement strong security practices and configure services with privacy and security in mind.

8. How long we keep your information

We keep your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and operate our business.
  • Comply with legal and accounting obligations.
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements.

When we no longer need your information, we will delete or anonymize it.

If you are a customer and you cancel your account, we may retain limited information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (for example, invoices and basic account records).

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights over your personal information, including:

  • The right to access and receive a copy of your data.
  • The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • The right to delete your data (subject to certain exceptions).
  • The right to object to certain types of processing or request restriction.
  • The right to data portability (receiving your data in a structured, commonly used format).
  • The right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at [privacy@yourcompany.com]. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

If you are in the EU/EEA or UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. Third-party websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any site you visit.

11. Children’s privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you through the website or by other means.

Your continued use of our website and services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Policy.
